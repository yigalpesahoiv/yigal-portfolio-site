About The Director

Yigal Pesahov

An award-winning filmmaker and director with over a decade of experience crafting cinematic narratives that transcend borders and cultures.

Based between Tel Aviv and Europe, Yigal specializes in luxury destination weddings and high-end commercial productions. His work has been featured in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and screened at international film festivals.

With a unique blend of documentary authenticity and editorial sophistication, Yigal captures not just moments, but the essence of human connection and emotion.
