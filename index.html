About The Director

Yigal Pesahov

Yigal Pesahov Films is a high-end production company specializing in commercial filmmaking, advertisements, corporate videos, music video production and luxury event cinematography. With 25 years of global experience, we bring cinematic storytelling to every project.

Our expertise spans corporate branding, high-impact marketing campaigns, luxury weddings and creative music video productions — all crafted with precision, emotion and cinematic depth.

We create immersive experiences that elevate brands, inspire audiences and deliver storytelling that resonates long after the screen fades to black.
